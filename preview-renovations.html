// The Process

How We Build

STEP 01

Discovery & Assessment

Walk your home with our team. We assess existing conditions, discuss your vision, identify structural opportunities and constraints, and establish a realistic scope.

STEP 02

Design Development

Floor plans, material selections, 3D renderings, and detailed specifications. You see every detail before demolition begins.

STEP 03

Permitting & Procurement

Building permits filed, materials ordered with confirmed lead times, and subcontractor schedules locked. No construction starts until everything is staged.

STEP 04

Selective Demolition

Careful removal of existing finishes and systems. We protect what stays and properly dispose of what goes. Discovery phase confirms structural assumptions.

STEP 05

Construction & Installation

Rough-in, framing modifications, finish installation. Weekly check-ins with photo documentation so you always know the status.

STEP 06

Final Reveal

Comprehensive punch list, final inspection, and walkthrough. Every detail reviewed, every surface cleaned, every system tested. Your transformed home, ready to live in.
